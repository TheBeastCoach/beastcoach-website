The Story

Me the Beast

Beast Coach was built by someone who has lived the frustration of fitness apps that don't actually understand how real life works.

The Founder

Built by Someone Who Needed It

I built Beast Coach because I got tired of fitness apps that suggested meals I didn't have ingredients for, workout plans that ignored my schedule and tools that treated nutrition like a spreadsheet.

I wanted something that felt like a personal coach — one that knew what was in my fridge, understood my goals and adapted to real life. Not a perfect week. Not an ideal scenario. Real life.

I also wanted something I could share with my partner. Fitness is genuinely more sustainable when someone else is on the journey with you. That meant building features that didn't exist anywhere else — a shared shopping list, a cycle tracker that both partners could see, and challenges you could compete on together.

That's Beast Coach. Built for real people. Built for couples. Built by someone who needed exactly this.

Our Mission

Make Fitness Accessible, Personal and Worth Sticking To

We believe fitness should work around your life — not the other way around. Beast Coach is built on the idea that the right tools, the right plan and the right support make all the difference between starting and actually finishing.
