The Founder

Built By Someone Who Has Been Where You Are

In 2003 Stefan Botha weighed close to 150kg. He lost a significant amount but for years remained stuck at around 25% body fat — training hard, eating reasonably well, but not moving the needle.


Six months ago he decided to make himself the priority. He studied labels, tracked everything, rebuilt his training from scratch and transformed his body at 48.


What he couldn't find was an app precise enough to match his exact preferences — one that connected his meal plan to his pantry to his shopping list and actually understood his goals. So he built it.

Smart Pantry

Everything Starts In Your Pantry.

The pantry is the engine of Beast Coach. Add ingredients and the app knows what you have. Your meal plan is built from what's actually in your kitchen. When you log a meal the ingredients are deducted automatically. When stock runs low it goes straight onto your shopping list.

  • Virtual pantry with full macro data per item
  • Physical pantry with low stock alerts
  • AI text, photo and barcode scanning to add items
  • Build a meal directly from pantry ingredients
  • Automatic stock deduction when meals are logged
  • Connects directly to your shopping list

Smart Shopping

The Shopping App That Keeps You Honest.

Your shopping list builds automatically from your meal plan and pantry stock levels, organised by aisle. Every item carries a health star rating. As you tick things off you can see the overall health score of your basket in real time. Use it at the checkout to see exactly what you're putting in your body before it gets home.

  • Auto-generated from meal plan and pantry
  • Organised by aisle — produce, meat, dairy, frozen
  • Live health score for your entire basket
  • Scan any item in store to add instantly
  • Tick off as you shop
  • Share the list with your partner
